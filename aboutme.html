About Me

I am a data analyst with experience in Tableau, R, SQL, Python, and Excel, holding bachelors degrees in Political Science and Economics from the University of Rhode Island along with a Data Analytics Certificate from the Community College of Rhode Island.

With a focus on extracting actionable insights from complex datasets, I excel at creating visually compelling dashboards and reports using Tableau. Through advanced data manipulation and modeling techniques in R, SQL, and Python, I uncover hidden patterns and correlations to uncover valuable information.

I am a collaborative team player who effectively communicates complex ideas to technical and non-technical stakeholders. By combining my technical abilities with my critical thinking skills, I deliver tailored analytical solutions that drive informed decision-making. Constantly staying updated with the latest trends in data analytics, I am passionate about leveraging data to make a real-world impact.
